Competitors

  1. Maps Platform (B2B API business)
    • Mapbox
    • HERE Technologies

    • OpenStreetMap (indirect, via ecosystem)

Competitors

  1. Business Listings / Google Business Profile layer
    • Yelp
    • Tripadvisor
    • Foursquare
    • Apple Maps listings
    • Facebook Pages
    • Instagram business profiles

Competitors

  1. Transactions & bookings
    • OpenTable / Resy (restaurants)
    • Booking.com / Airbnb (travel)
    • Uber / Bolt (rides)
    • DoorDash / Uber Eats (food delivery)
    • GetYourGuide / Klook (activities)

Competitors

  1. Mobility / Navigation / Traffic layer
    • Apple Maps
    • Regional apps (DeutscheBahn etc.)
    • Waze (internal but still competing for usage)
    • Citymapper
    • Moovit

Competitors

  1. Data & AI layer
    • Apple (privacy-first mapping stack)
    • HERE / TomTom (automotive + enterprise data)
    • Foursquare (location intelligence)
    • Uber (mobility data)
    • OpenStreetMap (community-driven data)
